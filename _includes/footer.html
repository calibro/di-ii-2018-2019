Calibro is a multidisciplinary design studio based in Milan, Italy interested in exploring new pathways at the intersection between design, data visualization and humanities to provide innovative and engaging visual tools for everyone, embracing complexity and aesthetics.

ISIA Urbino, Istituto Superiore per le Industrie Artistiche, is one of the most important public university in the field of communication and editorial design in Italy.
